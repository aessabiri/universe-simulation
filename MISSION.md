# 🎯 Your Mission: The First Cell

## Objective: Find a Habitable Planet and Initiate Evolution

### Step 1: Survive the Ignition
Begin your journey at the **Big Bang**. Free-look around the expanding multi-colored particle cloud. As the white-out flash subsides, navigate through the **Quark-Gluon Plasma** and watch the birth of the **First Stars**.

### Step 2: Navigate the Galactic Web
Advance the timeline to **Galaxy Formation**. Find the majestic spiral structures and explore the dense, dusty arms where solar systems are being forged.

### Step 3: Analyze Local Worlds
Enter the **Solar System**. This is your primary hunting ground. 
- Open the **Celestial Bodies** menu on the right.
- Click each planet to fly the camera to its orbit.
- Wait for the **Spectrometer HUD** to analyze the atmosphere.
- *Note:* Most worlds will be hostile. Look for high O2 and H2O signatures.

### Step 4: Deploy the Seed
Once you identify a world with >50% Habitability (**Earth**), the mission console will unlock the **[ DEPLOY BIOLOGICAL SEED ]** command. 
- Execute the deployment.
- The simulation will instantly lock onto that world.

### Step 5: Witness Evolution
Switch between the **Earth Eras** in the right-side menu.
- See the **Hadean Lava** cool under the fire of meteors.
- Watch the **Archean Oceans** settle.
- Survive the **Proterozoic Ice**.
- Finally, behold the **Modern Earth**—the successful result of your 13.8 billion-year journey.
