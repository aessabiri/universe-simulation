# 🌌 The Seeker Vision

## The Core Concept
The **Universe Simulation** is no longer just a passive visualizer. It is a narrative-driven **Astrobiological Quest**. 

You are the **Seeker**—a primordial, non-biological intelligence born in the fire of the Big Bang. Your purpose across 13.8 billion years is not merely to witness the formation of matter, but to find a rare, stable cradle where the miracle of biological life can begin.

## The Narrative Arc
1.  **Origin:** You begin at the singularity. You are pure energy, moving through the Quark-Gluon soup and the first stellar nurseries.
2.  **The Search:** As galaxies form and solar systems coalesce, you utilize your advanced sensors (The Spectrometer) to analyze the chemical makeup of new worlds.
3.  **The Goal:** Locate a planet with the perfect "Goldilocks" balance: liquid water, stable temperature, and a protective atmosphere.
4.  **The Sacrifice:** Once found, you deploy your "Biological Seed"—the fundamental building block of life—and watch as the planet transforms from a barren rock into a lush, blue marble.

## Philosophical Underpinning
This project explores the **Anthropic Principle**: the idea that the universe must have the properties which allow life to develop within it at some stage in its history. By putting the user in the role of the "Seed Ship," we make the vast scale of space and time feel personal and purposeful.
